# FirstPaper

**FirstPaper** is a visual novel and thesis-writing simulation built using **Ren’Py**.  
The game explores the **psychological and physiological struggles** experienced by final-year college students during the process of writing their thesis.

Rather than focusing only on academic tasks, *FirstPaper* simulates the human side of thesis writing — emotion, motivation, time management, and personal balance.

---

## About the Game

Writing a thesis is not just an academic challenge — it is a mental and physical journey.

In **FirstPaper**, players:

- Manage psychological states: Motivation that implements Self Determination Theory and Emotional states that implements J.A. Russell's Circumplex Model of Affect
- Balance physiological needs: Nutrition and Sleep that implements Circadian Rhythm
- Make decisions that influence productivity and well-being
- Experience consequences of burnout, procrastination, or discipline
- Navigate the emotional reality of final-year student life

The game aims to create an immersive and relatable experience for university students who are going through — or have gone through — the thesis-writing phase.
